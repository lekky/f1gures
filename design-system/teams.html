Constructor colors.

The 2026 grid. Team colour lives on the strip, the dot, the chip and the leader bar — never as a panel background. Every value below comes from src/data/buildFallback.js; the importer also harvests historical team colours from data/history for archive pages.

04 · Collisions & guardrails

Three team colours sit dangerously close to semantic tokens. When they appear together — chart legends, leaderboards — apply the disambiguation below.

TeamHEXCollides withDisambiguation rule
Ferrari#E80020--accent (#E8002D)In any UI showing both Ferrari and an active-state cue, route the active state to a 2 px underline + bold weight; reserve the red fill for Ferrari.
Kick Sauber#52E252--pos (#2ECC71)Never colour a Sauber driver's "position up" cell green; use the position cell colour (fg-1) and let the team strip carry the green.
Williams ↔ Racing Bulls#64C4FF / #6692FFEach otherAlways pair the strip with the team short-code (WIL / RBL) when both teams appear in the same view (e.g. season standings chart).
