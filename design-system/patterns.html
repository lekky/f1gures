01 · Records hero card

THE PATTERN TO FOLLOW

This is the colourful, dynamic card pattern the Records page is built from. Six anatomical pieces: eyebrow, big value, holder block (avatar + name + flag + context), top-5 bar chart with team-coloured fills, and an accent rule that hugs the card's top border on hover. Make every comparable card on the site behave like this one.
