A maiden Grand Prix win for <DriverChip ref="antonelli" /> at 19 years and
164 days. Pole, fastest lap, every lap led. The youngest race winner since
[Verstappen at Barcelona 2016](/races/2016/5/) and only the eleventh driver
in history to lead every lap of his first win.

## How it played out

Antonelli took pole by 0.084s from <DriverChip ref="russell" />, with
<DriverChip ref="hamilton" /> third on his Ferrari debut weekend at a circuit
he's won at six times. The sprint on Saturday went to Russell from Antonelli;
the order flipped on Sunday and stayed that way.

The race had two stories at the front. The Mercedes pair pulled away on the
opening stint and were never within a second of each other after lap 8 -
Antonelli's pace on the medium tyre was, by some margin, the fastest race
running of any 2026 car so far. Hamilton drove a quiet, controlled third for
Ferrari, his first podium in red. The result the move was made for.

Behind them, <DriverChip ref="leclerc" /> finished a frustrated fourth -
faster than Hamilton on long runs but bottled up by Charles' pace - and
<DriverChip ref="bearman" /> took an enormous P5 for [Haas](/teams/haas/),
his best result so far in F1.

## The McLaren disaster

<DriverChip ref="norris" /> didn't make the formation lap. A hydraulic fault
in the pit lane left him stranded; the car was wheeled away before lights out.
<DriverChip ref="piastri" /> qualified fifth, dropped to ninth on lap one in
contact with <DriverChip ref="gasly" />, and finished P19. McLaren scored
zero points from a weekend that started with both cars in the top six on the
grid. Two rounds in, [the team](/teams/mclaren/) has the worst points-per-grid
ratio in the field.

## The championship

After two rounds: Antonelli 43, Russell 43, Hamilton 27, Leclerc 27,
Bearman 16. Mercedes are level at the top of both championships. Red Bull
are fourth in constructors with 12 points - 8 from <DriverChip ref="max_verstappen" />'s
Melbourne recovery, 4 from <DriverChip ref="hadjar" />'s P8 in Shanghai.

[Japan](/circuits/suzuka/) in two weeks is the first high-speed aero test of
the new regulations.
