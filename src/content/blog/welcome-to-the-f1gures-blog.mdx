The f1gures blog is live. Alongside the live standings, calendar and 75 years
of archive data, we'll now be publishing longer-form writing in four buckets:

- **Race Recap** — what happened on Sunday, what it meant for the championship,
  and where the weekend was actually decided.
- **Technical** — how the cars work, why the regulations look the way they do,
  and the engineering stories behind a result sheet.
- **Driver Focus** — form deep-dives, career retrospectives, and the
  not-so-obvious numbers behind a driver's season.
- **Historic Season** — the seasons that shaped F1, mined from the full
  1950 onwards archive that the rest of the site is built on.

Posts will lean on the same dataset that powers the [driver pages](/drivers/),
[race pages](/calendar/) and [standings](/standings-drivers/) — so when we say
a result was a driver's best finish since X, the link goes straight to the page
where you can verify it.

[/blog/](/blog/). More soon.
